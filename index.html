About Us

Technight is an annual student-run hackathon based in Brooklyn, New York.
At TechHacks, we strive towards computer literacy and to expand student interest in technology and innovation.
an organization that has single handedly revived student interest
in programming in the Tech Triangle through its countless initiatives
and dedicated members. Whether you're just getting into programming or
have been programming for a while, you can join us at Technight and
have a good time!
