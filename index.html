About


Technight is an annual student-run hackathon based in Brooklyn, New York.
At Technight, we hope to improve students' interest in programming solutions.
At Technight, we help connect hackers together through teamwork and pizza.
Join us this year as we propel technology to new levels and our creative minds.
We hope to see you at Technight and have a worthwhile experience.

FAQ


Where is Technight?

Technight is graciously hosted @The Bushwick Generator.
It is located here @ 215 Moore St, Brooklyn, NY 11206.

When is the deadline to sign up?

The deadline to sign up will be March 15th, 2019

Who can attend?

While Technight is only for high school and middle school students, anyone else can sign up to be a mentor.

What do I need?

You need to bring a laptop, a charger, your school ID and a sleeping bag (if you plan to sleep during the event).

When does Technight start?

Technight starts at 9:00 AM on April 13th, and ends at 1:00 PM on April 14th.

Do I need experience in coding?

Experience is not necessary. If need be, our mentors and workshops will help you throughout the event.
